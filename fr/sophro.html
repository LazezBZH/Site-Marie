Sophrologie

What is sophrology.

Created in the 60 by Alfredo CAYCEDO Neuro­psycholo­chiatriste, sophrology is inspired by several disciplines such as hypnosis, and eastern techniques such as yoga and zen. A real mix of eastern and western technique.

Sophrology it’s a discipline that help every one of us to develop to have serenity and well-being through personal development based techniques that will affect the body and the mind .It combines exercises that s focus on breathing and concentration, muscle relaxation and mental imagery (also visualisation).

Sophrology is:

  • A simple and practical tool to reap the benefits of meditation anywhere, anytime
  • Quick, guided and easy exercises for body and mind – requiring only 10 – 15 mins a day
  • Helps to deal withstress,anxiety, embrace restfulsleep,performat your best and thrive in modern life
  • Meets you where you are in your self-development journey and help you further transform without long analysis
  • Helps you connect more to your body and power your inner world to guide you in your life
  • Helps you cultivate the things that truly matter and build a positive future

WHO IS IT FOR?

Activity based on “Sophrology for Life” for children, adolescents, adults and the elderly. Accessible to all, sophrology adapts perfectly to the individual’s needs using simple and effective exercises.

How is a Sophrology session for your child?

Sophrology sessions for children can take place in an individual or collective setting. The sessions are focused on the playful side.At the start of the session, we start with a discussion and sharing with the child.Then come practical exercises of relaxation, breathing, listening to the body, imagination, awakening through games,stories, movements ... At the end of the session, we can have a dialogue or use the drawing.
